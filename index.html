Clémence Chanavat

I am a PhD student in math = computer science, at the TallCat lab in Tallinn, under Amar Hadzihasanovic. I am currently working on importing the theory of directed algebraic topology into the framework of diagrammatic sets. My main interests are category theory, homotopy theory and their applications to computer science.

During the year 2021-2022, I was a student at the LMFI Master where I learnt about theoretical computer science, category theory, homotopy theory, and logic in general. Since February 2023, I am a Ph.D student.
